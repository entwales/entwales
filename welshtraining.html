"In 2008 I entered Higher Surgical Training in Otolaryngology in Wales. The experience, professionally and personally, was strongly positive and led to a happy and seamless progression to being a young Consultant in Wales. During training the Consultant body were approachable, affable and expert. The surgical exposure was broad and complex and by the end of the training programme I had completed more than 100 major head and neck cases, more than 100 major rhinological cases and more than 100 mastoid cases as principal surgeon. Living in Wales is marvelous - the natural beauty of the country is well recognised and happily ubiquitous; the people are kind and humorous; and the cities are thriving. Finally, and most importantly, the training was genuinely 'family-friendly' and never did I feel any pressure to put work ahead of family. Over a decade later, I am a young Consultant in Wales and wholeheartedly recommend the country as a place to train, live and progress.”

Sam Fishpool
Consultant at Royal Glamorgan Hospital
